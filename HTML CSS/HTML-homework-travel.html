Travel to Thailand....

First reason to travel to Thailand

The first reason is that Thailand is a very diverse country. Everything that you do in Thailand is determined by where you want to go. if you go north you will have to do activities that suit the north's mountainous terrain. if you are going south you have to change the activities to become more suited to the south's border to the ocean. This is also the same for all the other areas in thailand.

Second reason to travel to Thailand

It is really easy to travel in Thailand. The easiest ways to travel are:

  1. BTS,
  2. MRT,
  3. bicycle
  4. skate board
  5. by Foot
  6. Motorcycle,
  7. and finally the easiest way to travel which is accesible anywhere on a road, Taxi!
